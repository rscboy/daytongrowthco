# What We Build | DaytonGrowthCo.

> DaytonGrowthCo builds websites, SEO systems, phone agents, quote tools, dashboards, customer portals, automations, and custom apps for small and midsized businesses.

## From online presence to the systems behind the work

DaytonGrowthCo helps small and midsized businesses improve how customers find them, how requests are handled, and how the team delivers the work without paying traditional custom-development prices.

## Core Capabilities

### Build your presence

When a business is difficult to understand online or does not look as capable as it is in person, DaytonGrowthCo builds modern websites, service pages, sales pages, and website care systems.

### Get discovered

When customers are searching but the business is hard to find or hard for AI systems to interpret, DaytonGrowthCo improves SEO, answer engine optimization, local search signals, useful content, and focused campaigns.

### Capture and schedule

When calls go unanswered or intake details are lost before work is booked, DaytonGrowthCo builds phone agents, request forms, scheduling flows, and follow-up systems.

### Run the work

When quotes, files, customer updates, and project details are spread across too many tools, DaytonGrowthCo builds quote builders, dashboards, customer portals, training systems, and custom apps.

## Common Systems

- Phone agents and AI intake systems
- Quote calculators and estimate builders
- Project dashboards and staff dashboards
- Customer portals and upload workflows
- Training libraries and SOP portals
- Website design, local SEO, and answer engine optimization
- Sales pages, proposal pages, product visuals, and custom apps

## Cost Positioning

DaytonGrowthCo uses AI-assisted development to reduce the build hours a traditional custom development shop would bill for. The result is custom-fit software and websites designed for small-business budgets.
