# Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: March 2026

### Our Commitment

DaytonGrowthCo. is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and aim to apply relevant accessibility standards to our digital properties.

---

### Standards We Follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including those with visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological disabilities.

---

### Ongoing Improvements

We view accessibility as an ongoing effort. We actively monitor our website and implement updates to improve navigation, readability, and compatibility with assistive technologies such as screen readers and keyboard navigation.

---

### Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some third-party content or tools embedded on our site (such as payment processors or booking widgets) may not fully meet accessibility standards. We are actively working with our vendors to address these limitations.

Encountered a barrier?

We welcome your feedback. If you encounter an accessibility issue on our site, please contact us and we will evaluate and resolve it promptly.
