About Us

Meet the Founder

Born and raised in Dayton, Ohio, Samuel Caruso has always loved building things.

He earned his undergraduate degree in History from the University of Michigan and later completed a Master's in Entrepreneurship and Emerging Technology.

"Most businesses don't need another website. They need something that actually works."

After seeing how many businesses struggled with outdated websites, Samuel realized the problem wasn't design - it was that their websites didn't actually do anything.

That insight became the core of DaytonGrowthCo.: we don't just build websites - we build tools that help your business get customers and run more efficiently.
