Common questions

What software do roofing companies actually need?

Most roofing companies do not need a giant platform. They need a few connected tools: a way to capture calls and photos, a quote builder that uses their real pricing, and follow-up that does not depend on memory. DaytonGrowthCo builds those around the crew's existing workflow.

Can you build a roofing quote tool from our own price sheet?

Yes. A roofing quote builder uses your real pricing, materials, and rules so estimates come out consistent and send-ready. It turns call notes, photos, and measurements into a clean quote priced the way you already price.

How do you stop missed calls from becoming lost roofing jobs?

An AI phone agent answers when the crew is on a roof, asks the job questions, captures the address and details, and books or escalates. Your team gets a clean summary instead of a voicemail to chase later.

How much does custom software for a roofing company cost?

Websites start at $1,500 and custom tools are quoted to scope. AI-assisted development keeps the cost well below a traditional dev shop, and most first builds go live in two to four weeks.
